FAQ

Will the film make my windows dark or mirror-like?

No—there are multiple grades, from near-clear to higher privacy. We choose what fits your look and comfort goals.

Is it safe for double-pane or insulated glass?

Often yes, but it depends on the glass type and sun exposure. We assess and specify the right film to reduce thermal-stress risk.

When will I feel the difference?

Heat and glare reduction is noticeable quickly. Slight initial haze can occur and typically clears as the film cures.

How do I clean it?

Use a soft cloth and non-abrasive cleaner. Avoid harsh tools, especially in the first days after installation.

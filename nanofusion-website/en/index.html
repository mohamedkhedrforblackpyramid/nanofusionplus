Special instructions after the installation of insulation film and paint protection film services

First: Heat Insulation Films

  • If bubbles, air pockets, or separation at the edges appear, the branch must be contacted within 24 hours of installation for inspection and repair.
  • Do not open the windows for 48 hours after installation, and ensure the vehicle is exposed to sunlight.
  • Do not clean or wipe the inside of the glass for one week.
  • Do not use the defogger heavily for two weeks.

Second: Paint Protection Films

  • Do not wash the vehicle for one week after installation, and ensure it is exposed to sunlight.
  • You must visit the branch within one week of installation for a technical inspection and to ensure proper adhesion.
  • Do not use the vehicle in harsh weather conditions until the inspection is complete and the film is fully dry and adhered.

Care and maintenance instructions

To ensure optimal performance and longevity of your NANOFUSION product, please adhere to the following:

  • During the first week after installation, allow the adhesive specifically designed for the film to fully bond to the glass or paint.
  • Do not use liquids containing alcohol, ammonia, dyes, chemicals, or acids.
  • Remove contaminants (such as insects or bird droppings) immediately to avoid permanent stains.
  • High-pressure washers are not recommended.
  • It is recommended to use a soap specifically designed for car washing.
  • Use clean water and soft, lint-free cloths.
  • It is recommended to use a rubber squeegee or a soft, dry cloth when drying.
  • Do not use paper towels as they contain abrasive fibers.
  • Do not apply stickers or adhesive strips to the film.
  • Do not peel or remove the film edges to avoid damaging the adhesive system.
Important note
  • Failure to follow the above instructions will automatically void the warranty.

Note regarding appearance after installation

  • A hazy appearance or water bubbles may appear between the film and the windshield after installation. This is normal and temporary due to the moisture used during installation.
  • This appearance will gradually disappear within four (4) to ten (10) weeks.

🚘 Tips & Care Guide – Nano Fusion

Tips and care guidance to keep your vehicle in the best condition. At Nano Fusion, our relationship doesn’t end after installation—it begins. Follow these recommendations to maximize the performance of PPF and window insulation films and preserve a premium finish.

🛡️ Aftercare for Paint Protection Film (PPF)

⏳ First 7 days

  • Avoid washing the vehicle
  • Do not press on or attempt to adjust the film
  • Avoid direct exposure to high‑pressure water

Tip: the film needs time to fully settle and bond.

💦 After one week

  • You may wash the vehicle normally
  • Hand washing with dedicated products is recommended
  • Avoid harsh brushes or careless automatic washes

⚠️ Avoid

  • Strong chemicals
  • Non‑specialized polishing
  • Peeling or removing the film manually
🌡️ Window tint (insulation film) aftercare

🕒 First 48 hours

  • Do not open the windows
  • Avoid touching the film from the inside

🔆 Key tips

  • Clean using a soft cloth only
  • Use ammonia‑free cleaners
  • Avoid sharp tools
🚿 Best way to wash your car

To preserve protection quality:

  • Use car‑specific shampoo
  • Wash with a soft microfiber cloth
  • Dry the vehicle immediately after washing
  • Avoid washing under direct sunlight

💎 Proper washing = longer film life + better appearance.

☀️ Tips to maintain insulation performance
  • Park in shaded areas when possible
  • Use an additional front sunshade
  • Keep glass clean consistently
⚡ How to keep a premium look
  • Clean the vehicle regularly
  • Treat contaminants immediately (especially bird droppings)
  • Don’t leave dirt for long periods
  • Visit for periodic inspection and maintenance
🧠 Pro tips from Nano Fusion
  • Protection isn’t neglect—care preserves quality
  • The better you maintain it, the higher the resale value
  • Original products + professional installation = best results
❗ When should you come back?
  • If bubbles appear or you notice anything unusual
  • If the vehicle experiences strong friction/impact
  • For periodic inspection or questions
